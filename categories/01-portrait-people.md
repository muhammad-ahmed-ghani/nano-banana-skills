# Category 1: Portrait & People Photography

Templates for generating portraits, selfies, headshots, and people-focused images with Nano Banana.

---

## 1.1 Professional Headshot / Business Photo

**When to use:** User wants to convert a casual selfie into a professional profile photo.

```
Keep the facial features of the person in the uploaded image exactly consistent. Dress them in a professional [navy blue / charcoal / dark suit] with a [white / light blue] shirt. Background: Place the subject against a clean, solid [dark gray / '#562226' / navy] studio photography backdrop with a subtle gradient, slightly lighter behind the subject and darker toward the edges (vignette effect). No other objects.

Photography Style: Shot on a Sony A7III with an 85mm f/1.4 lens, creating a flattering portrait compression. Lighting: Classic three-point lighting setup. The main key light creates soft, defining shadows on the face. A subtle rim light separates the subject's shoulders and hair from the dark background.

Crucial Details: Render natural skin texture with visible pores, not an airbrushed look. Add natural catchlights to the eyes. The fabric of the suit should show a subtle [wool / linen] texture. Final image: ultra-realistic, 8K professional headshot.
```

**When to use:** User wants an infographic-style beauty or age analysis overlaid on a portrait.

```
Use the provided portrait photo as the base. Do NOT change the person's face, expression, age, skin tone, or gender. Just overlay a clean, minimal infographic on top.

Create a high-resolution vertical "FACIAL AESTHETIC REPORT" poster, studio lighting, soft beige background, premium beauty clinic style. Add thin white lines and labels pointing to each area of the REAL face, with percentage scores based on global aesthetic ratios, symmetry, and proportions:

1. Eyes: "Eyes Beauty – [X]%"
2. Cheeks: "Cheeks Harmony – [X]%"
3. Lips: "Lips Shape – [X]%"
4. Eyebrows: "Eyebrows Design – [X]%"
5. Jaw & Chin: "Jaw & Chin Definition – [X]%"
6. Overall Facial Symmetry: "Facial Symmetry – [X]%"

At the bottom center: large bold "OVERALL SCORE: [X]%" inside a circle.

Design style: clean, medical-grade, aesthetic-clinic infographic. Modern thin sans-serif typography. White text and lines, subtle drop shadows. No logos or extra graphics beyond the labels and scores.
```
