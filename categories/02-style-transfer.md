# Category 2: Style Transfer & Transformation

Templates for converting between styles — anime/realistic, cyberpunk, vintage, art movements, and era transformations.

---

## 2.1 Live-Action to Anime Style

**When to use:** User wants to convert a real photo into anime/manga aesthetic.

```
Show me the animation style image version of the uploaded photo.
```

**Extended version:**
```
Transform the uploaded [photo / image] into a [Studio Ghibli-inspired / Makoto Shinkai / classic 90s anime / modern anime] illustration. Preserve the person's facial structure and key identifying features. Render in [soft watercolor washes with ink outlines / cel-shaded with bold line art / painterly digital illustration]. Maintain the original composition, lighting direction, and emotional tone. Background: stylized [matching the original setting / simplified into abstract color fields / detailed anime environment].
```

---

## 2.2 Anime to Live-Action Conversion

**When to use:** User wants a 2D anime character rendered as a real person.

```
Show me the real-life photo version of the anime character in the uploaded image.
```

**Extended version:**
```
Create a photorealistic, live-action version of the anime character from the uploaded image. Translate their defining features — [hair color, eye shape, outfit details, distinguishing marks] — into realistic human proportions and textures. Photography style: [cinematic portrait / fashion editorial / candid street photo]. Lighting: [soft studio / golden hour / dramatic side lighting]. The result should feel like a professional photograph of a real person who embodies this character, not a cosplay — a true real-world equivalent. 8K, hyperrealistic skin texture.
```

## 2.4 Era Aging / Temporal Consistency

**When to use:** User wants to see a person at different ages, or generate a "through the ages" progression.

```
Generate [a holiday photo / portrait / candid scene] of this person through the ages up to [60 / 70 / 80] years old. Maintain perfect facial consistency — same bone structure, same identifying features — at each age. Show [4 / 6 / 8] stages: [ages 5, 15, 25, 40, 55, 70 / childhood, teens, young adult, middle age, elderly]. Each stage: appropriate aging signs [crow's feet, grey hair, posture shift], era-appropriate clothing and setting, consistent warm-toned photography style. Arrange as a [grid / timeline strip / single composite].
```

## 2.5 Traditional Art Style Transformation

**When to use:** User wants a photo or scene rendered in a classical or cultural art style.

**Chinese Gongbi (fine-line) style:**
```
A traditional Chinese ink and color painting in Gongbi style on aged rice paper texture. [SUBJECT DESCRIPTION — e.g., "A noblewoman in elaborate Tang Dynasty Hanfu robes sits on a wooden stool"]. [ACTION — e.g., "holding a modern smartphone taking selfies"]. Background features classical gnarled pine trees, bamboo groves, and Taihu rocks. Traditional Chinese calligraphy written in the top right corner, accompanied by a red artist chop seal. Color palette: muted mineral pigments. [Humorous anachronistic fusion / reverent classical rendering]. --ar 16:9
```

**Japanese Ukiyo-e style:**
```
[SCENE] reimagined as a traditional Edo-period Ukiyo-e woodblock print. Flat perspective, bold outlines, limited color palette of [indigo, vermillion, gold, black]. Characteristic Hokusai-style [waves / figures / landscape elements]. Fine hatching for shadow and texture. Aged paper texture with subtle foxing.
```

**Watercolor + Ink:**
```
[SUBJECT] rendered in a delicate watercolor-ink style. Fine ink outlines with soft, bleeding color washes. [Muted greys and blues / warm earth tones / vivid jewel tones] dominate. Visible paper grain adds to the texture. Expressive, slightly loose brushwork that still captures likeness. White space used intentionally for balance.
```

## 2.8 Hyperrealistic Anime Portrait with Dramatic Lighting

**When to use:** User wants an anime character or person rendered with dramatic, editorial lighting.

```
Generate a hyperrealistic realistic-anime portrait of [a female / male] character standing in a completely black background.

Lighting: Use a narrow beam spotlight focused only on the center of the face. The edges of the light must be sharp and dramatic. All areas outside the spotlight should fall quickly into deep darkness (high falloff shadow), almost blending into the black background. Not soft lighting.

Hair: [long dark hair with some strands falling over the face. The lower parts of the hair should fade into the shadows / specific hair description].
Pose: [one hand raised gently to the lips in a shy, hesitant gesture. Eyes looking directly at the camera with a mysterious mood / alternative pose].
Clothing: [black long-sleeve knit sweater; the sweater and body mostly disappear into the darkness / clothing description].

Overall tone: dark, moody, dramatic, mysterious. High-contrast only in the lit portion of the face. Everything outside the spotlight should be nearly invisible.
```
