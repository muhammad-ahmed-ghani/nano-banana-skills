# Category 6: E-commerce & Product Photography

Templates for product shots, ad recreation, fashion/lifestyle imagery, and virtual studio photography.

---

## 6.1 Luxury Floating Product Shot

**When to use:** User wants a high-end commercial product photograph with dramatic environment.

```
Product:
[BRAND] [PRODUCT NAME] — [bottle/package shape], [label description], [liquid/material color]

Scene:
Luxury product shot [floating on dark water / suspended in mid-air / resting on marble] with [flower type — e.g., white gardenias / red roses / cherry blossoms] in [colors] arranged [around it / behind it / cascading from above]. [Lighting style — e.g., "golden hour glow" / "cool morning mist" / "bright fresh studio light"] creates [reflections and ripples across the water / dramatic shadows / clean highlights].

Mood & Style:
[Ethereal and luxurious / Fresh and clean / Dark and mysterious / Bold and vibrant]. High-end commercial photography. [Camera angle — e.g., "45° above / eye-level / low dramatic angle"]. Shallow depth of field with soft bokeh background. [Color palette to emphasize: deep jewel tones / soft pastels / monochromatic].
```

## 6.2 Ad Recreation with Different Product

**When to use:** User wants to swap their product into an existing ad concept or scene.

```
Recreate this ad concept using my product instead of theirs. Keep the same mood, lighting, composition, and overall vibe. Remove all competitor branding.

Replace the product with the one from my uploaded image and adapt all background elements, props, and surrounding textures to be visually relevant to my product's brand, [flavor profile / color story / brand personality] and aesthetic — not the original brand's. Maintain photorealism and accurate product proportions. [Final aspect ratio: 1:1 / 4:5 / 16:9].
```

## 6.3 Virtual Try-On / Fashion Lifestyle Shot

**When to use:** User wants to show clothing or accessories on a model in a realistic setting.

```
Keep the facial features of the person in the uploaded image exactly consistent. Style them in [PRODUCT DESCRIPTION — e.g., "a fitted emerald green wrap dress with gold button accents" / "the uploaded streetwear outfit"]. Setting: [an upscale urban street at golden hour / a minimalist white studio / a cafe terrace].

Photography: [Editorial fashion / Candid lifestyle / E-commerce clean background]. Camera: 50mm f/1.8, natural light + [reflector fill / ring light / strobe]. The clothing should be presented clearly with accurate [fabric texture / color / fit details visible]. Model pose: [confident and direct / candid walking / seated casually]. 8K, commercial quality.
```

## 6.6 Compact Camera / Nostalgia Product Framing

**When to use:** User wants to show a photo as displayed on a vintage or specific camera screen.

```
Use facial features of the attached photo. A close-up shot of [a young woman / person] displayed on the screen of a compact [Canon / Nikon / Sony] digital camera. The camera body surrounds the image with its buttons, dials, and textured surface visible — including [the FUNC/SET wheel, DISP button, and IMAGE STABILIZER label / other camera-specific details].

The photo on the screen shows the person [indoors at night / outdoors in daylight / in a social setting], illuminated by a [bright built-in flash that creates sharp highlights / soft natural light / warm indoor glow]. [Hair description], [expression description]. Background: [dim kitchen with appliances / bedroom / street scene] softly blurred.

Mood: candid, raw, nostalgic, reminiscent of [early 2000s / 1990s] digital camera snapshots. Colors: slightly muted with [cool undertones / warm film tones], strong flash contrast, natural grain from the display. No text or logos inside the photo preview itself.

Camera simulation: compact digital, equivalent 28–35mm, f/2.8, ISO 400, 1/60s with flash, auto white balance.
```
